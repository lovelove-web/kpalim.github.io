Welcome to SDA Kpalime Zomayi

We are a community of believers preparing for the Second Coming of Jesus Christ. Founded on Biblical teachings, we worship every Saturday, the Sabbath, and hold various services and activities to strengthen our faith and share the gospel with the world.

Our Beliefs

As Seventh-day Adventists, we believe in:

  • The Trinity (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit)
  • Baptism by immersion
  • The Sabbath as a day of rest and worship
  • Holy Communion
  • The Bible as the inspired Word of God
  • 28 Fundamental Beliefs

Our mission is to preach the gospel and prepare everyone for the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Meeting Schedule

We invite you to join us during the following services:

  • Sabbath Worship: Every Saturday
    • Sabbath School (Bible Study)
    • Divine Service (Sermon)
    • Afternoon Bible Study
  • Prayer Meeting: Every Wednesday, 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Vesper Service: Every Friday, 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM
